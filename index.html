About

I study advanced semiconductor devices through physics-based TCAD simulation, focusing on Gate-All-Around (GAA) nanosheet FETs at 4.2 K, where classical device models break down and freeze-out, band-tail conduction, and trap-induced noise take over. Supervised by Dr. Navjeet Bagga.

Research

  • Cryogenic device simulation — GAA nanosheet FETs to 4.2 K, freeze-out, incomplete ionization, SS saturation.
  • Reliability & noise — RTN and NMP charge trapping with trap-location dependence in 3D.
  • Radiation effects — SET/SEU in stacked nanosheets, including cryogenic soft errors.
  • Compact modeling — cryo-aware BSIM-CMG/IMG capturing band tails and trap noise.
